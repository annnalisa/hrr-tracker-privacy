HRR Tracker — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026  |  Effective date: March 17, 2026

Summary: HRR Tracker does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. All information you enter stays on your device only. We have no servers, no accounts, and no access to your data — ever.

1. About This App

HRR Tracker ("the App") is a personal fitness tracking application developed and owned by Annalisa Nash Fernandez ("we," "us," or "our"). The App allows users to log Heart Rate Recovery (HRR) data after exercise sessions for personal health tracking purposes.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with the App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your heart rate data or any health metrics you enter
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device identifiers, IP address, or advertising IDs
  • Your usage patterns, session duration, or app behavior
  • Any photographs, contacts, or other device content
  • Any biometric data of any kind

The App does not require you to create an account. There is no login, no registration, and no user profile of any kind.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Android's standard local storage system (AsyncStorage):

  • Heart rate values you manually enter (peak HR, recovery HR, resting HR)
  • Activity type and session details (duration, perceived effort)
  • Date and time of each logged session
  • Your calculated HRR scores and zone classifications
  • A flag recording that you have accepted this disclaimer (no personal data)

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us, to any third party, or to any server of any kind. It is stored solely for the purpose of displaying your history and analytics within the App.

You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the App.

4. No Third-Party Services

HRR Tracker does not integrate with, transmit data to, or receive data from any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, cloud storage providers, or social media platforms.

The App does not contain advertisements of any kind.

5. No Internet Connection Required

The App functions entirely offline. It does not require an internet connection to operate, and it does not make any network requests during normal use.

6. Health Data Disclaimer

HRR Tracker is a fitness tracking tool for personal, informational use only. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

Heart Rate Recovery data provided by the App is for general informational purposes only and should not be interpreted as medical advice. Always consult a qualified physician or cardiologist before beginning, modifying, or stopping any exercise program, particularly if you have or suspect a heart condition or any other health concern.

We expressly disclaim any liability for decisions made based on data or information provided by this App.

7. Children's Privacy

HRR Tracker is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used this App, please contact us at the email address below and we will take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.

Because the App does not collect any personal data, changes to this Policy are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.

9. Your Rights

Because HRR Tracker does not collect or store any personal data on our systems, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or delete. All data associated with the App resides solely on your device and is fully within your control.

To delete all App data, simply uninstall HRR Tracker from your device.
