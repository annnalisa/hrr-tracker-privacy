1. About This App

HRR Tracker ("the App") is a personal fitness tracking application developed and owned by Annalisa Nash Fernandez ("we," "us," or "our"). The App allows users to log Heart Rate Recovery (HRR) data after exercise sessions for personal fitness tracking purposes only.

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with the App. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Policy and to the Health Disclaimer and Liability Waiver set forth herein.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your heart rate data or any health metrics you enter
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device identifiers, IP address, or advertising IDs (these may be collected by Google AdMob — see Section 5)
  • Your usage patterns, session duration, or app behavior
  • Any photographs, contacts, or other device content
  • Any biometric data of any kind

The App does not require you to create an account. There is no login, no registration, and no user profile of any kind.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using standard local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Heart rate values you manually enter (peak HR, recovery HR, resting HR)
  • Activity type and session details (duration, perceived effort)
  • Date and time of each logged session
  • Your calculated HRR scores and zone classifications
  • A flag recording that you have accepted this disclaimer (no personal data)

You can delete all stored data at any time by uninstalling the App.

4. No Internet Connection Required for Core Features

All core features of the App — logging sessions, viewing history, and calculating HRR scores — function entirely offline and make no network requests. An internet connection is only used to load advertisements served by Google AdMob as described in Section 5.

5. Advertising — Google AdMob

HRR Tracker displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service operated by Google LLC. Google AdMob may collect and use certain information from your device in order to serve relevant advertisements, including:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA)
  • IP address and general location information
  • Device type, operating system, and app usage data
  • Interaction data with ads displayed within the App

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC under its own privacy policy, which you can review at policies.google.com/privacy. We do not receive, access, or control any data collected by Google AdMob.

On Apple iOS devices, the App will request your permission to track via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before any advertising identifier is accessed. You may decline this permission and the App will continue to function normally, though ads may be less relevant.

We do not sell your data to advertisers or any third party. We do not share your health data with Google AdMob or any other party under any circumstances.

7. Apple App Tracking Transparency (iOS Only)

On iOS devices, Apple requires apps that access advertising identifiers to request your explicit permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. When you first open HRR Tracker on an iOS device, you will be asked whether to allow tracking for advertising purposes. This permission relates solely to Google AdMob's advertising identifier access. Your health data is never involved in this request and is never shared with any advertiser regardless of your choice.

You may change your ATT preference at any time in your iPhone's Settings app under Privacy & Security → Tracking.
